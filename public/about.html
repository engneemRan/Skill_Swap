    About Skill Swap

    Skill Swap is a collaborative platform designed to help people share and exchange skills with each other.
    Whether you're a professional wanting to teach your expertise, or a learner eager to grow — Skill Swap connects you with the right people in your community.

    Our mission is to make learning more accessible, personalized, and community-driven — because everyone has something valuable to share, and something new to learn.
